About This Diagram

This Venn diagram illustrates the hierarchical relationship between Artificial Intelligence (AI), Machine Learning (ML), and Deep Learning in healthcare contexts. Deep Learning is a specialized subset of Machine Learning, which itself is a subset of the broader field of Artificial Intelligence. This nested structure helps clarify how these technologies relate to each other and enables various healthcare applications including natural language processing, computer vision, predictive analytics, recommendation systems, and knowledge graphs.
